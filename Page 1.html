The Start

You are dispatched to the small town of "y". The queen has dispatched with with instructions to build the towns defenses, because the fearsome dragon "z" will attack it in one year. As the bravest, and most valiant knight in the land, you, "x", are tasked with building "y"'s defenses and saving the lives of thousands of villagers.

As you approach Portlandia on horse, a thought strikes you. You don't even know where the best place to start building the defenses is! You are faced with your first choice as the savior of "y". What is going to be more important to you-learning about the physical nature of the town, and its myraclass natural defenses, or talking to the villagers and getting to know them?
